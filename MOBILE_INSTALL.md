# Installing MultiChat on Your Mobile Phone

MultiChat can be installed on your mobile phone just like a regular app! Follow these simple steps:

## For Android (Chrome/Edge)

1. **Open MultiChat in Chrome or Edge**
   - Go to your MultiChat website URL (e.g., `https://yourdomain.com`)

2. **Install the App**
   - Tap the menu button (⋮) in the top right
   - Select **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
   - Tap **"Install"** in the popup

3. **Launch MultiChat**
   - Find the MultiChat icon on your home screen
   - Tap to open - it will run like a native app!

## For iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1. **Open MultiChat in Safari**
   - Go to your MultiChat website URL

2. **Add to Home Screen**
   - Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow pointing up)
   - Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
   - Edit the name if desired
   - Tap **"Add"**

3. **Launch MultiChat**
   - Find the MultiChat icon on your home screen
   - Tap to open!

## Benefits of Installing

- **Quick Access**: Launch directly from your home screen
- **Full Screen**: No browser chrome, more screen space
- **App-Like Experience**: Works just like a native app
- **Offline Support**: Basic functionality even without internet

## Troubleshooting

### "Add to Home Screen" option not showing?

**Android:**
- Make sure you're using Chrome or Edge browser
- Check that the site is served over HTTPS
- Try refreshing the page

**iOS:**
- You must use Safari browser (Chrome/Firefox won't work)
- Make sure you're not in Private Browsing mode

### App won't open after installation?

- Try uninstalling and reinstalling
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection

## Uninstalling

**Android:**
- Long-press the MultiChat icon
- Select "Uninstall" or drag to "Remove"

**iOS:**
- Long-press the MultiChat icon
- Tap "Remove App" → "Delete App"

## Notes

- The app requires an internet connection for most features
- Your data is stored on the server, not on your phone
- Updates happen automatically when you open the app
- The app uses minimal storage on your device
